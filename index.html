About Enigma 2.0

Enigma 2.0 is a modern tribute to the legendary Enigma machine used during World War II. While the original Enigma was eventually broken, this modern version incorporates contemporary cryptographic principles and technologies.

Our mission is to educate people about the importance of encryption in protecting privacy and securing communications in the digital age.

This project combines historical significance with modern web technologies to create an interactive learning experience.
